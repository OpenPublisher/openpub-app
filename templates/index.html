Open Publisher

an open-source protocol for decentralized scientific publishing

Instantly publish and receive permanent proof-of-ownership of your data without the need for a third-party publishing company.
Powered by the Ethereum blockchain and IPFS file-sharing network.

I am comfortable with blockchains

Open Publisher is a permissionless Ethereum protocol which enables instant censorship-resistant publishing. When a user submits data to be published, the data is cryptographically hashed and written to an ERC-721 PUB token which is distributed to the submitter. Posession of PUB tokens allows the user to participate in governance of the protocol.

I am new to blockchains

Open Publisher is a new system for publishing in which researchers instantly recieve a permanent digital reciept upon submission of their data. This receipt stores a unique fingerprint of their data so they can prove ownership of it even after it's released to the public. Users can then use this receipt to propose changes to the network and vote on proposals from other users.
